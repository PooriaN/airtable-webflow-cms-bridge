Database connection

CMS Bridge cannot boot without PostgreSQL, so DATABASE_URL or POSTGRES_URL must be configured before the dashboard can sync data. You can use Supabase, Render Postgres, or your own PostgreSQL instance. On local setups this usually lives in your .env.
